Privacy Policy

App: Mindset Shift
Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mindset Shift ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application, including when you connect your TikTok account.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Mindset Shift to access your TikTok account via TikTok Login Kit, we may receive the following information, limited to the scopes you approve:

  • Basic profile information (display name, username, avatar) — user.info.basic
  • Permission to upload video content to your account — video.upload
  • Permission to publish video content on your behalf — video.publish

We do not collect passwords. Authentication is handled entirely through TikTok's official OAuth 2.0 flow.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information and permissions described above are used solely to:

  • Generate and upload short-form video content to the connected TikTok account.
  • Display basic account information for verification purposes during setup.

3. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored locally and securely, and are used only to communicate with TikTok's official API endpoints. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

4. Data Retention

Access and refresh tokens are retained only for as long as necessary to operate the app's publishing functionality, or until you revoke access via your TikTok account settings.

5. Your Rights

You may revoke Mindset Shift's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok's app settings ("Manage apps and devices"). You may also request deletion of any data we hold by contacting us using the information below.
